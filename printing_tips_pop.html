DART Printing Tips

To print the calendar on a color printer, follow the steps below on how to set your browser.

  • FIREFOX /MOZILLA - click on Select File >> Page Setup... and other Options. Then check the box marked Print Background (colors & images). 
  • NETSCAPE NAVIGATOR - click on Edit, Preferences, Appearance, and then Colors. Locate the section that says, Sometimes a document will provide its own colors and background, and if it is not already selected, click on Always use the colors and background specified by the web page is selected. Then click on Okay.
  • APPLE SAFARI - you should not have to change any settings to print in color.
  • INTERNET EXPLORER - click on Tools, Internet Options, and then Advanced. Scroll down to Printing and if it is not already selected, click on Print Background Color and Images. Then click on Okay> at the bottom of the menu page. 

Note: We design for Firefox, so we that is the recommended browser. We do not recommend Internet Explorer due to problems with table layout and printing.
