Graduation Progress Report - [name] - [school]
The Bering Strait School District requires specific courses in nine "content areas" or subjects to be completed to a level of Proficient or higher for a student to graduate. In addition, passing scores are required on all three sections of the Alaska High School Graduation Exit Exam (HSGQE). The report below shows your progress toward graduation. The levels highlighted by tiny stars are the minimum graduation level, and you must have an overall score of Proficient or Advanced for those. For each 25% of the total standards in a course, you are awarded one quarter of the available credit for that course. Core courses are worth a miximum of 1 credit, and Foundation courses are worth .50 credit.
